Ingredients

  • 1 Package Instant Ramen (Chicken or Shrimp flavor)
  • 1/4 Tbsp salted butter
  • 2 1/4 cups water
  • 1 egg
  • 1 stalk of celery
  • 1/8 tsp LouisianaTM original hotsauce

Steps

  1. Pour water into saucepan and heat on high until boiling
  2. Melt butter over medium heat and fry egg to desired doneness
  3. Add instant ramen to boiling water and boil until 2 minutes before the noodles reach your desired doneness
  4. Wash and chop celery into bite size pieces
  5. 2 minutes prior to noodles reaching your desired doneness, add instant ramen seasoning, then pour ramen soup into bowl and top with fried egg, hot sauce, and chopped celery
  6. Enjoy!
